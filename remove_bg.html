About Eden Tools Background Remover

Free AI Background Remover — by Eden Tools

Eden Tools offers a fast, free AI-powered Background Remover — no registration, no ads, no limits. Upload any image and get a clean transparent PNG in seconds, ready to use anywhere.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Background Remover free?

Yes — 100% free, no registration, no ads, and no limits. Eden Tools is free for everyone, forever.

What image formats are supported?

JPG, PNG, and WEBP images are supported. The output is always a transparent PNG file.

Does it work on mobile?

Yes. Eden Tools works in any mobile browser — Chrome, Safari, Firefox — without installing any app.

Is my image stored or shared?

No. Your image is processed instantly and is never stored or shared on our servers.

How accurate is the AI background removal?

The AI works best on images with a clear subject — portraits, products, and objects against distinct backgrounds. Results are clean and ready to use in seconds.
