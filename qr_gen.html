About Eden Tools QR Code Generator

Free QR Code Generator — by Eden Tools

Eden Tools offers a fast, free QR Code Generator — no registration, no ads, no limits. Paste any URL or text and get a clean, scannable QR code in seconds. Download it as a PNG with a white border, ready to print or share.

Works on any device — Android, iPhone, tablet, or desktop — right in your browser. No app to install.

What can I use it for?

You can encode website links, Wi-Fi passwords, contact info, phone numbers, plain text, or anything else into a QR code. The generated code can be scanned by any standard camera app or QR scanner.

✅ Trusted by users worldwide — completely free.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Eden Tools QR Code Generator free?

Yes — 100% free, no registration, no ads, and no limits. Eden Tools is free for everyone, forever.

What can I encode in a QR code?

Anything — website URLs, Wi-Fi credentials, phone numbers, contact cards (vCard), or plain text messages.

Can I download the QR code?

Yes. After generating your QR code, click the Download PNG button to save a high-quality image with a clean white border.

Does it work on mobile?

Yes. Eden Tools works in any mobile browser — Chrome, Safari, Firefox — without installing any app.

How do I scan the QR code I created?

Open your phone's camera and point it at the QR code. Most modern phones will automatically detect and open the link or text.
